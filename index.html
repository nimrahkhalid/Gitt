Taj Mahal

This is an image of the Taj Mahal, one of the most iconic monuments in the world, located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India.Built between 1632 and 1653 by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. The Taj Mahal is an extraordinary example of Mughal architecture, combining elements of Indian, Persian, and Islamic styles.

Machu Picchu, Peru

This image features Machu Picchu, a 15th-century Inca citadel nestled high in the Andes Mountains of Peru. The site is shrouded in mist with the iconic Huayna Picchu mountain rising prominently in the background. Terraced fields and stone structures are visible, showcasing the advanced engineering of the Inca civilization. Location: Cusco Region, Peru, approximately 2,430 meters (7,970 ft) above sea level. Historical Significance: Built around 1450, Machu Picchu was likely an estate for the Inca emperor Pachacuti. It remained unknown to the Spanish during the conquest and was rediscovered by Hiram Bingham in 1911.
